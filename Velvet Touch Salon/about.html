The Velvet Philosophy

Founded in 2018, Velvet Touch Salon was born from a simple belief: everyone deserves to experience true luxury in their hair care journey.

We've curated an environment that speaks of relaxation and sophistication. From the moment you step through our doors, you are treated not just as a client, but as a canvas for our artistry.

Our continuous commitment to education ensures that our team is always at the forefront of global hair trends, bringing you the latest techniques combined with timeless elegance.
