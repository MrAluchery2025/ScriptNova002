Privacy Policy

A plain-language overview of what information ScriptNova collects and how it is used.

Information we collect

We collect the details you choose to share through the contact form, such as your name, email address, company, budget range, and project message.

How inquiry data is used

Inquiry details are used to review project fit, respond to you, and manage client conversations. We do not sell your personal information.

Analytics and performance

Basic website analytics may be used to understand traffic and improve the site experience. This helps us evaluate which pages are performing well and where visitors drop off.

Data retention

Project inquiries are retained only as long as reasonably needed for communication, record keeping, or business operations.

Your choices

You can request an update or deletion of the personal information you submitted by emailing hello@scriptnova.com.
