Springlights Arts Hub

Explore every club where creativity meets opportunity in Voi.

Painting

Freedom, discovery, and bold self-expression. We provide canvases and brushes for imagination to flourish.

Dance

From afro-fusion to ballet basics and hip-hop. Warm-ups for strength, choreography for teamwork.

Drama

Training covers voice, diction, character work, and improvisation. Explore African folklore and contemporary themes.

Coding

Logic through games and apps. Building websites, chatbots, and robotics projects for future-ready learners.

Photography

Framing, lighting, and storytelling through lenses. Practical sessions on nature walks, studio shoots, and school events.

Engineering

Building prototypes for bridges, catapults, and robots. Hands-on science with real-world impact for tomorrow's builders.

Skating

Balance, stride, and fitness. Safety-first training for beginners in our structured weekend program.

Drawing

Pencil, charcoal, and ink techniques to train the eye. Portfolios track growth from roughs to refined style.

Designing

Blending fashion and graphic basics. Mood-boarding, pattern ideas, and logo thinking for industry-ready creativity.

Ballet

Building strong foundations with grace, grit, and growth. Focus on alignment and elegant stage presence.
