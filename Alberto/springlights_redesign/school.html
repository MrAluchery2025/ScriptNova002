Springlights Music School

Learn instruments, discover your talent, and grow with the rhythm of excellence.

Piano

The piano builds a strong foundation for all music. Students gain rhythm, melody, harmony, and coordination.

Violin

The violin teaches precision, ear training, and discipline. It nurtures confidence and artistic expression.

Saxophone

The saxophone introduces jazz, blues, and modern sounds. Students explore improvisation and expressive performance.

Recorder

Recorder is the gateway to woodwinds. It teaches breathing, note reading, and rhythm. Perfect for young beginners.

Choir & Ensemble

Build teamwork, harmony, and vocal strength. Singing together creates unity and joy in music.

Music Software

Learn to compose, mix, and produce with industry tools. Software training equips you for modern music creation.
