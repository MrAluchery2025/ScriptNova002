How We Work

The Roadmap

A systematic, four-phase approach that transforms your vision into a high-performance digital product — on time, on spec, and beyond expectation.

Four Phases. Zero Guesswork.

Every engagement begins with deep understanding and ends with measurable outcomes.

01

Discovery

We begin with an in-depth stakeholder workshop to map your business objectives, audience personas, and competitive landscape. This phase produces a detailed project brief, success metrics, and a shared vocabulary for the engagement. Typical duration: 1–2 weeks.

02

Drafting

Our design team translates strategy into wireframes, high-fidelity mockups, and a complete component library. Every screen is reviewed against accessibility standards and conversion best practices before a single line of code is written. Deliverables include a clickable Figma prototype and a design system document. Typical duration: 2–3 weeks.

03

Engineering

We build in weekly sprints with continuous client-facing staging environments, so you can review progress at every milestone. Code is authored for performance — sub-2s Time to Interactive is our baseline target. All code is version-controlled, documented, and delivered with full handover materials. Typical duration: 4–8 weeks.

04

Launch & Growth

Deployment is supported by a rigorous QA checklist covering cross-browser compatibility, WCAG 2.1 AA accessibility, Core Web Vitals, and SEO. Post-launch, we monitor analytics, resolve any issues, and can transition into a retainer relationship for ongoing feature development and growth strategy.
